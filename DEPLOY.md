# Deployment Guide for TRON Shasta Testnet & Mainnet

## Prerequisites
- Ensure you have the TRON wallet installed.
- Make sure you have the TRONlink extension.
- You will need at least 1 TRX for deploying contracts on the mainnet (testnet deployment does not require TRX).

## Deployment Steps for TRON Shasta Testnet
1. **Set Up Your TRON Wallet:**  
   - Download and install the TRON wallet.
   - Create or import your wallet using the TRONlink extension.

2. **Connect to Shasta Testnet:**  
   - Open your TRON wallet and switch to the Shasta test network.

3. **Get Test TRX:**  
   - Use the TRON Shasta faucet to get test TRX for deploying your contracts.

4. **Prepare Your Smart Contract:**  
   - Write your smart contract code using Solidity.

5. **Compile Your Smart Contract:**  
   - Use the TRON smart contract compiler to compile your Solidity code.

6. **Deploy Your Smart Contract:**  
   - Go to your TRON wallet and navigate to the contract deployment section.
   - Select your compiled contract and hit the deploy button.

7. **Verify Your Contract Deployment:**  
   - Check for your contract address in the TRON explorer for the Shasta testnet.

## Deployment Steps for TRON Mainnet
1. **Set Up Your TRON Wallet:**  
   - Download and install the TRON wallet.
   - Create or import your wallet using the TRONlink extension.

2. **Connect to Mainnet:**  
   - Open your TRON wallet and switch to the main network.

3. **Fund Your Wallet:**  
   - Ensure your wallet has at least 1 TRX for deploying contracts.

4. **Prepare Your Smart Contract:**  
   - Write your smart contract code using Solidity.

5. **Compile Your Smart Contract:**  
   - Use the TRON smart contract compiler to compile your Solidity code.

6. **Deploy Your Smart Contract:**  
   - Go to your TRON wallet and navigate to the contract deployment section.
   - Select your compiled contract and hit the deploy button.

7. **Verify Your Contract Deployment:**  
   - Check for your contract address in the TRON explorer for the mainnet.

## Conclusion  
- You have successfully deployed your smart contract on both the TRON Shasta testnet and mainnet. Happy coding!
